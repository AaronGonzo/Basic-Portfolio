About Me

I am an Arizonian and grew up in Phoenix. I went to Arizona State University for my Bachelors Degree in Tourism and Management with a minor in Special Events Management and it was then that I found a love for travel. After I graduated from University, I started my first job working at STA Travel as a travel expert. While working there I helped students go on an exchange program to my dream destinations, Australia and New Zealand. About a year of working at STA Travel I decided it was time to take a leap and purchase a one way ticket to Fiji and Ausrtalia. I lived there for a year and then came back to Arizoa for a year and decided I wanted to move to Spain and teach english. I figured out all the paperwork, got the visa and went to Malaga, Spain. I spent two years there and decided I wanted to come back and do coding bootcamp so that I can continue to travel but have a better remote type job.
